Frequently asked questions

Are there any limits on the number of courses, students, or admins based on pricing?
No, Frappe Learning offers unlimited courses, students, and admins on all plans. We dont believe in restricting growthwhether youre running a small training program or a large learning platform, you get full access without per-user pricing.
How can I get consultation?
You can connect with one of our official partners for consultation and implementation support.
What is Priority SLA?
Priority SLA includes faster response and resolution time as mentioned in Table II of our Service Level Agreement (SLA)
Do you have discounts for education institutes and non profits?
Yes, we support education institutes and non profits. Contact us to apply the discount.
Can I monetize my courses?
Yes, Frappe Learning allows you to set up paid courses using the Monetization feature. You can integrate a payment gateway, collect payments, and start generating revenue from your content.
Is there a free plan available?
Yes! You can self-host Frappe Learning for free since it's fully open-source. If you prefer a managed solution, you can host it on Frappe Cloud, starting at just $5/month.
I have more questions.
Drop us a message here and our team will get in touch with you!
